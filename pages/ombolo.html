Ombolo

Ombolo is a specific pork cut. You can fry it or steam if it is fresh or dried. This recipe will boil it first an dfry it later.

You can add some wine at the end of cooking. Be careful when it evaporates, because it is extremely flammable. But if you want this can make a Flambé effect

Ingredients

  • ombolo
  • olive oil

  • Optional ingredients

  • Bread
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Wine

Steps

  1. Cut the meat in slices of equal width.
  2. Add the meat to the pan and just enough water to submerge the slices.
  3. Turn the heat on and boil the meat.
  4. When the water runs out and the meat is sticking to the pan, add oil.

    5. Best practice: move the pan from the fire when adding oil, because it can light a fire.

  5. Turn the meat and evenly fry both sides

    6. If you want to: add wine to the pan.

  6. Eat with bread.
