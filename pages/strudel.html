Strudel

Strudel is a typical dish from areas of the former Hungarian Empire. It has thin foil and apples. You can add cinnamon and sugar.

The best foil is homemade foil. Don't buy junk at the store.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Vinegar
  • Apples

    • To your liking

  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Prepare warm water and add salt, oil and vinegar.
  2. Add half of the flour and mix for 4'.
  3. Add all the flour and knead for 10'.
  4. Let the dough rest for 60'.
  5. In the meantime cut the apples and cook them on medium heat with some butter.
  6. Shred some dry bread and add it to a pan.
  7. Add butter to the bread and cook it until it looks orange.
  8. Cook the apples until they are soft.
  9. Wait for the apples to cool down.
  10. Flatten the dough and stretch it using your knuckles.
  11. Cover the foil with a thin paste of bread and all the apples.
  12. Heat the oven on 180˚ on ventilated mode.
  13. Roll the foil and move it on a plate
  14. Move the plate in the oven and cook for 30'.
  15. Bon appétit.
